About the Game

Adventures With Anxiety is an innovative interactive story game designed to help players understand and manage anxiety through immersive gameplay. Created by Nicky Case, this game combines storytelling with practical mental health insights.

The game follows a character dealing with anxiety in various everyday situations, allowing players to make choices that influence the story's outcome. Through this interactive approach, players learn valuable coping strategies and gain a deeper understanding of anxiety disorders.

This isn't just entertainment – it's a tool for mental health awareness and education, making complex psychological concepts accessible through engaging interactive media.
